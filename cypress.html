Cypress & 5th

Cypress&5th is basically an E-commerce website for the women's. The website gives a plenty of options for their users some of them are :

  • Choose a Box containing offers and a surprise with each box to make their customers smile with each box they choose. The bigger the box, the bigger is the surprise.

  • Help women get access to the latest personalized fashion trends and inspirations with an unparalleled level of service. As a result, selected assortment of in season, on-trend clothing for the users to pick from each month.

  • A lot of pretty stuff to choose from the boutique section.

  • Buy a gift card and you will get credit to shop for what you Love

Problem Statement :

Create a complete responsive e-commerce website by giving them the best experience of shopping. The Website should have the following features(web pages):

  • Blog: Design up a custom blog page for cypress users so that they can see the latest activities/trends from the cypress team

  • Homepage: The homepage should contain the basic information about the website with a custom navigation bar. So that the users can know what they can expect from the cypress.

  • Box: Cypress provides 3-4 boxes. Each of the boxes will have their own benefits as per the selected box you will get some surprise from the Cypress team.

  • Boutique: Boutique page is the page where the users can view the products provided by the cypress, which includes the accessories, clothing, shoes and other stuff. In the aside, there is a custom filter by which the users can check out for the products they want without having the hesitation of scroll all the way down to check out a product.

  • Product Modal: You can view any product by clicking on the eye icon or just click on the image. This modal contains a variety of information such as the product image(with zooming facility to have a deep look at the product), Product title, Offer price, Customize your order(select the size, select a color) and product description. If you are done with the customization process and liked your product you can simply add the product to the bag.

  • Cart: The cart section is basically divided into the two sections. left part describes shows the selected products. You can increase the quantity of the product along with removing a product. the right section provides the brief billing details and the option of checkout.

  • Checkout: The checkout page contains the checkout details also there is a section for the shipping details. In case the shipping and the billing details are same them you can simply select the checkbox. By clicking on that icon your billing details are automatically merged into the shipping details. While on the right section you will have a brief summary and the payment method section. And a button to place an order.

  • Order history: The order history page will have the following entities: Order ID, Date Added, No. of products, Customer, Total amount, Status (Delivered, Cancelled, processed, Payment Failed). Also on the right, there is a section which contains the shortcut links such as My Account, Edit Account, Password, Gift Cards, Transactions, Logout.

  • Order Details: This page contains all the information about the order that has been placed. like order details, address details, product detials and the amount to be paid.

  • Order Return:On the order return page you will have the liberty to return and keep products from the selected box or custom order from the boutique section. All you need to do is select the product you wish to keep or return. The amount will be deducted according to the selection made and you can easily return the selected items.

  • Profile Questionnaire: These are a set of questions by which the cypress can check out your taste and then shows you the products according to your taste.

  • Gift cards: This section gives you the best deals at the best prizes. Buy a gift card and you will get credit to shop for what you love

  • Connect: Have any styling questions? You will get all your answers. Whether you have a question about how to pick your first date outfit or how to pair patterns and colors. Also, you can get connected to cypress&5th via social media and check out the latest updates.

  • Login / Sign up: Login to check out the things from where you left.

  • Account Recovery: Enter your email and you can get help to get access to your account.

  • About Cypress: How all this started.

Design and code a complete responsive e-commerce website for women to make the shopping experience less hashy.
