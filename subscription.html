Subscription v1

The Aim of subscription v1 is to give special features to scroll+ users to have a hassle free news without any ads at a very low rate.

Goals :

  • Enable subscription option for Scroll.in readers

  • Session-based features on/off (eg. ads)

  • Secure setup

  • Logically and optionally physically separate components

  • Using third-party providers whenever possible to cut down dev work

  • Free readers (Will see all ads and can't use personalized features)

  • Free subscribers (Signed up readers. Will see ads)

  • Paid subscribers (Paid for one of the plans)

Team :

4-5 People (Includes PM's, Developer, Product design lead)

Technologies Used :

Sketch, Invision, HTML, Sass, Javascript

Role :

My role in this project not just stays with designing things, I have to take the full ownership of the project from research to help engineers built this amazing product. There are certain steps which I've followed :

  • Research: The beginning of everything… target audience, goals, tasks, context, meaningful requirements, etc.

  • Discuss: Discussion with the product managers and engineers is what I feel is the crucial part of my design process as I get to know what actually we are going for and to check the possibilities from the engineers. In this Process, I get to ask a lot of questions to have a clear thought process.

  • IxD & Wireframing: Affordances, cues, CTAs, visual elements – everything is put onto the interface blueprints in order to engage a user, motivate him to interact, and ensure positive emotional feedback afterward.

  • Design and Prototype: While designing a product I make sure that it's pixel perfect and clean enough that even a kid could navigate through the interface. Once I'm done with designing I Prototype (HTML/CSS or Invision) to give the designs a life.

  • Testing: Check if I missed anything? Once everything is ok the prototype, Sketch File, invision board with the flow is shared with the engineering team.
